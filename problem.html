Welcome to the Kotilo problem-solving section. Here you will find a set of problems aimed at improving waste management and promoting better environmental practices. Each problem is designed to challenge your creativity and help you think of innovative solutions.

By participating, you are not only helping the environment but also contributing to a larger movement toward sustainable living. Explore the problems below and take the first step towards making a difference.

Problem 1

Despite the importance of waste sorting, many consumers remain unaware of the proper methods for separating their trash. This lack of knowledge leads to contamination in recycling bins, rendering valuable materials unusable.

Problem 2

Plastic pollution is a pressing issue affecting our ecosystems and health. Millions of tons of plastic end up in landfills and oceans each year, causing harm to wildlife and contributing to environmental degradation.

Problem 3

Organic waste constitutes a significant portion of the trash that ends up in landfills, contributing to greenhouse gas emissions. Encourage local communities to adopt composting practices to divert organic waste from landfills
